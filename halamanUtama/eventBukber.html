The Togetherness of Class S12B During Iftar Moment


On a warm evening during the month of Ramadan, the students of Class S12B came together to celebrate a special Iftar gathering. The event was not just about breaking the fast, but also about strengthening friendships, building memories, and enjoying a sense of unity among classmates.

With laughter echoing in the air and delicious traditional dishes served, everyone shared stories, experiences, and joyful moments. It was a chance to relax from daily routines, connect on a deeper level, and appreciate the values of togetherness and gratitude.

Organized by the class committee, the gathering reflected the spirit of cooperation and harmony that defines the Student Life Hub. It was a meaningful experience that reminded everyone of the importance of unity and celebration during special times.
