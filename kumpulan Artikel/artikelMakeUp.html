Makeup in 2025: Beyond Beauty—A Lifestyle of Expression and Care

Written by Group 1 — Thematic Focus: Beauty, Expression & Self-Care · June 2025

Makeup in 2025 has transformed from a beauty tool into a cultural force. No longer confined to vanity, it's now a form of emotional self-care, personal branding, and creative expression used by people of all genders and backgrounds.

What Is Makeup?

Makeup is the application of cosmetic products to create a desired look—whether subtle, bold, artistic, or experimental. Products include primer, foundation, concealer, eyeshadow, eyeliner, mascara, blush, bronzer, and lipstick. Each serves to enhance features or express mood and identity.

2025 Trends: Blending Beauty and Identity

  • Glowy Skin: Emphasizes health with tinted moisturizers and liquid highlighters.
  • Monochrome Makeup: Harmonizes eyes, lips, and cheeks in one color palette.
  • Statement Eyes: Bright shades and graphic liners steal the spotlight.
  • Hybrid Beauty: Skincare-infused makeup like SPF foundations are now standard.

Why People Wear Makeup

Makeup boosts confidence, allows self-expression, and creates quiet moments of personal care. Whether applying mascara before class or creating an elaborate look for an event, it’s a ritual that connects people with their inner selves.

"Makeup is my therapy. It's how I face the world," says Ayu, a student makeup artist in Jakarta.

Tips for Everyday Use

  • Moisturize before applying foundation for smooth blending.
  • Use concealer only where needed for a natural look.
  • Pick lipstick shades based on your outfit or mood.
  • Always finish with a setting spray to keep your look fresh.

Makeup Tips by Skin Type

Oily Skin

Use a matte primer and oil-free foundation, and set with powder. Carry blotting papers for quick fixes.

Dry Skin

Start with a rich moisturizer. Use hydrating foundation and liquid highlighter. Avoid heavy powders.

Combination Skin

Use different primers for oily and dry zones. Balance products to match your overall skin texture.

Sensitive Skin

Stick to hypoallergenic, fragrance-free makeup. Use gentle essentials like BB cream and tinted lip balm.

Normal Skin

Feel free to explore! Maintain balance and adjust your routine based on the occasion.

Universal Guidelines

  • Never sleep with makeup on.
  • Clean brushes and tools regularly.
  • Apply sunscreen before your makeup in the morning.

Makeup for All

The makeup world is now open to everyone. Men, women, and non-binary individuals use it for professional polish, gender expression, or artistic freedom. Inclusivity in product ranges is no longer an exception—it’s an expectation.

In 2025, makeup isn’t about masking flaws—it’s about showing up authentically, one brushstroke at a time.
